    • Specify acceptable behavior for users while interacting with the chatbot.

  5. Data Privacy and Security:

    • Detail how user data is collected, processed, and protected.
    • Mention compliance with relevant privacy laws (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).

  6. Liability and Disclaimers:

    • Limit your liability for any damages arising from chatbot use.
    • Include disclaimers about accuracy, reliability, and fitness for specific purposes.

  7. Termination and Suspension:

    • Explain conditions under which you can terminate a user’s access to the chatbot.
    • Describe the process for suspension or removal.

  8. Governing Law and Jurisdiction:

    • Specify the governing law (usually the jurisdiction where your business is based).
    • Address dispute resolution mechanisms (e.g., mediation, arbitration).
